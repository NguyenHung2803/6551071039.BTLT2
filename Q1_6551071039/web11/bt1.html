eCommerce

Ecommerce of electronic ecommerce, a subset of ebussiness, e-commerce is the purcharsing, and exchanging of goods and service over computer network (such as the internet) through which transuction or terms of sale are perforned electronically.

eCommerce requirements
Shopping cart Merchant account
Processing gateway Digital Certificate

Ecommerce can be broken into four main categories

  • C2C(Consumer-toConsumer)
  • B2C(Bussiness-to-Consumer)
  • C2B(Consumer-to-Bussiness)
  • C2C(Consumer-toConsumer)
